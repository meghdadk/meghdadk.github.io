About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Warwick working under the supervision of Prof. Peter Triantafillou. The focus of my PhD research has been on the concept of model updatability within the field of Machine Learning. I have delved into two main areas of study within Machine Learning known as continual learning and machine unlearning. Continual learning involves understanding how to acquire new knowledge without erasing previously learned information, while machine unlearning explores the process of intentionally forgetting specific information previously learned. Throughout my PhD journey, I have engaged with a wide array of machine-learning paradigms, models, and methodologies.
